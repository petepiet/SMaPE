SMAPE (Symple Midi and Playstyle Extractor) - Analysis Disclaimer
==================================================================

This .symple bundle was generated by SMaPE, an automated tool that extracts
MIDI and fingering data from piano performance videos using computer vision
and audio transcription.

IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS:
======================

1. ACCURACY: The tool uses AI-powered hand tracking (MediaPipe) and automatic
   music transcription (Kong AMT). Both are high-quality but imperfect.
   Errors can occur, especially with:
   - Poor video quality, lighting, or camera angle
   - Occluded hands or fingers
   - Fast passages or complex polyphony
   - Non-standard hand positions or playing techniques

2. MANUAL REVIEW REQUIRED: Before using this fingering for performance,
   teaching, or publication, manually review the suggestions. The tool's
   recommendations are a starting point, not definitive.

3. VIDEO DEPENDENCY: Results quality directly depends on your video:
   - Steady camera (minimal motion/zoom)
   - Overhead angle with full keyboard visible
   - Good lighting on hands and keys
   - Performance must match the MIDI exactly

4. PEDAL DATA: The tool extracts sustain pedal events (CC64) from MIDI
   transcription. These reflect the audio analysis, not video tracking.

5. NOT FOR CRITICAL USE: Do not rely on this tool for medical, accessibility,
   or safety-critical applications.
